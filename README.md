## üìå Overview
The **AI-Powered Healthcare Awareness Chatbot** provides **verified, reliable, and accessible** health information via natural conversation. It uses **ChatGPT, NLP, and Machine Learning** to combat misinformation and promote preventive healthcare.

## üö® Problem Statement
Millions of people rely on misinformation, unsafe self-medication, and unverified online sources, leading to delayed treatment and preventable diseases. Barriers include:

- Low health literacy
- Language differences
- Limited 24/7 healthcare access
- Overloaded healthcare systems
- Mental health neglect

## üéØ Solution
The chatbot provides **real-time, accurate, and user-friendly responses** to health queries:

- Symptoms, diseases, and first aid
- Prevention and vaccination
- Nutrition, lifestyle, and mental wellbeing
- Local health alerts

It serves as a **digital health companion**, accessible anytime, anywhere.
